console chromatic guitar tuner helps getting musical instruments in tune.
It is ad-free, straightforward and intuitive. Only a few MB of storage are needed.
The reference frequency A4 can be chosen from 437 to 443 Hz.
Audio driver, algorithm and some other things can be adjusted to your instrument in the settings.
The deviation from the next correct pitch is show as flowing histogram and in the color of the pitch name.
Additionally the octave can be shown.
